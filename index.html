Hi there.

I'm Kersten.

I live in the digital city of Darmstadt and work as a consultant and developer with over 4 years of professional experience.

I work as a freelancer and spin-off partner for various companies and agencies.

I advise my clients exclusively for their own benefit and always consider all options.

I love to use my knowledge and experience for new projects - but the real attraction for me is to always learn new things and to face the individual challenges of each new project.

My focus is clearly on web development, the rapidly evolving technology in this area demands everything from developers, who stops, quickly loses the connection. I have specialized in NodeJS and its ecosystem, I have recently found great favor with VueJS and Nuxt.

Nevertheless (or just because of that?) I always look beyond the end of my nose, keep an eye on new technologies and try to take a holistic approach to projects, to think along with everything that could have an influence on the result.
